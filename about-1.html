TripleO (OpenStack On OpenStack) is a program aimed at installing, upgrading and operating OpenStack clouds using OpenStack's own cloud facilities as the foundations building on nova, neutron and heat to automate fleet management at datacentre scale.

The World #RunsOnOpenStack Thousands of users around the world run on OpenStack. Discover why organizations adopt OpenStack, what workloads they are running and the impact it has on their overall business.

This presentation will focus on our journey from 3 borrowed servers connected to users via CAT6 cables running through a drop ceiling to a 2000-core cluster backed by a 4PB Ceph array that is 100% code-driven.  We will also discuss our CI pipeline that integrates our Blackboard LMS, Heat, and our asciidoc-based curriculum-as-code repository into a student experience that is always up-to-date, relevant, and technically accurate while investing minimum man-hours for content curation.  This DevOps-oriented approach, underpinned by OpenStack, allows our instructors to focus on teaching students and creating new content, not creating slide presentations and formatting word processor documents.  This approach has yielded high benefits in courseware quality and student outcomes while saving millions of dollars in platform TCO expenses.  The OpenStack solution has put OSS  ahead of similar government education programs and on track to continue to embrace innovation and change for the benefit of security.

Methods - Cluster the server (Linux/RedHat),
Install the OpenStack on the server and then virtualize the instance and install the second instance of OpenStack  over the server

Intended outcome :
Able to create our internal cloud in the campus and access according to our own privileges
